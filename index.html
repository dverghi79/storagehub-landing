Step 1

Connect storEDGE

Enter your storEDGE API credentials. StorageHub pulls occupancy, tenant, and revenue data from every facility in your account automatically.

Step 2

Link QuickBooks

One-click OAuth connection to QuickBooks Online. StorageHub maps PMS revenue entries to your chart of accounts automatically. No manual journal entries.

Step 3

See your portfolio

Within minutes, your unified dashboard shows occupancy, revenue, delinquencies, and activity across every facility. Updated hourly, reconciled automatically.

Step 4

Get alerted proactively

Delinquency alerts, revenue discrepancies, and occupancy drops trigger notifications before they become costly problems. No more discovering issues a month late.
