The Problem

Multi-site operators are drowning in disconnected tools

You open storEDGE for occupancy data. Then QuickBooks for revenue. Then the gate system for access logs. Then a spreadsheet to try to reconcile it all. By the time you have a clear picture of your 7 facilities, 45 minutes are gone, and the numbers still do not match.

Delinquent tenants slip through the cracks because no single system tracks the full picture. You miss revenue leaks because QuickBooks and your PMS report different numbers. And when you want a cross-facility view of how the portfolio is doing, you are back to spreadsheets.

How It Works

Connected in minutes, not months

01

Connect storEDGE

Enter your storEDGE API credentials. StorageHub pulls occupancy, tenant, and revenue data from every facility in your account.

02

Link QuickBooks

One-click OAuth connection to QuickBooks Online. StorageHub maps PMS revenue entries to your chart of accounts automatically.

03

See your portfolio

Within minutes, your unified dashboard shows occupancy, revenue, delinquencies, and activity across every facility. Updated hourly.

SiteLink integration coming soon. storEDGE + QuickBooks available at launch.
